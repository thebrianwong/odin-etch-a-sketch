Etch-A-Sketch

Paint on the pixel canvas. Choose your paint color by clicking one of the colors on the bottom.

Change the number of grids in each row and column by clicking on the "Change Number of Pixels" button. Then enter a number from 1 to 100.

Erase the canvas by clicking on the "Reset Canvas" button.

Modes

  • Free Draw: Paint freely with your available colors of choice.
  • Random Color: Each pixel you paint will be a random color. Who knows what color you'll get!
  • Monochrome: Paint, but in black and white!
  • Shade In: Rub the canvas with your mouse until it's fully black!
  • Automatic Random Color: No painting here, just enjoy the light show! WARNING: your browser might lag if there's too many pixels.
  • Automatic Moving Pixel: Send a pixel on a journey from the top of the canvas to the bottom. Click the button multiple times to send even more!
